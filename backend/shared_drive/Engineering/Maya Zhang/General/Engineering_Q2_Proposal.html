Executive Summary

The Engineering Department has achieved significant revenue growth, with a 15% increase in total revenue compared to the same quarter last year. This growth is attributed to the successful completion of several projects, including the Customer Portal Redesign and API Infrastructure Upgrade.

Our profit margin has also seen an uptick, standing at 42.67%. However, we recognize that this increase is partially due to a one-time expense related to the project upgrades.

Despite these challenges, our team remains committed to delivering high-quality projects and driving growth for the company.
