    • Key Concepts and Principles:

    Design thinking is a human-centered approach to problem-solving that involves empathy, creativity, and experimentation. The key concepts and principles include:

    • Empathy: Understanding the needs and feelings of users through observation, interviews, and other research methods. 2. Definition: Defining the problem or opportunity in a clear and concise manner. 3. Ideation: Generating a wide range of ideas and solutions through brainstorming and mind mapping. 4. Prototyping: Creating tangible representations of ideas to test and refine them. 5. Testing: Validating solutions with real users through usability testing, A/B testing, and other methods. 6. Iteration: Refining and iterating on solutions based on feedback and results.
