**Key Concepts and Principles**

Design thinking is a human-centered approach to problem-solving that emphasizes empathy, creativity, and experimentation. The core principles of design thinking include:

  • Empathy**: Understanding the needs, pain points, and motivations of your target audience 2. **Define**: Defining the problem or opportunity you want to address 3. **Ideate**: Generating a wide range of ideas and possibilities 4. **Prototype**: Creating a tangible representation of your idea 5. **Test**: Testing and refining your prototype with feedback from stakeholders

**Key Principles:**
