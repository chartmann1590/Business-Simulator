**Key Concepts and Principles**

Design thinking is a human-centered approach to problem-solving that emphasizes empathy, creativity, and experimentation. The following are the key concepts and principles:

  • Empathy**: Understanding the needs, desires, and pain points of customers through research and observation. 2. **Define**: Defining the problem or opportunity through customer discovery and understanding. 3. **Ideate**: Generating a wide range of ideas and solutions through brainstorming and creative thinking. 4. **Prototype**: Creating a prototype or proof-of-concept to test and refine ideas. 5. **Test**: Testing and validating the solution with customers and stakeholders.
