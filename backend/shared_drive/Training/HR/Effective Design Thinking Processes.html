**Key Concepts and Principles**

Design thinking is a human-centered approach to problem-solving that involves empathy, creativity, experimentation, and iteration. The key concepts and principles of design thinking include:

  • Empathy**: Understanding the needs, wants, and behaviors of users or customers 2. **Ideation**: Generating and exploring ideas for solutions 3. **Prototyping**: Creating tangible representations of ideas to test and refine them 4. **Testing and iteration**: Continuously evaluating and refining solutions based on user feedback

    • Design thinking is a process that involves:

    • Defining the problem**: Identifying the challenge or opportunity 2. **Researching and empathizing**: Gathering insights from users or customers 3. **Ideating and prototyping**: Generating and testing ideas 4. **Testing and iterating**: Refining solutions based on user feedback
