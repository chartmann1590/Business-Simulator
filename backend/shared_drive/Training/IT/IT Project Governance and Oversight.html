**Key Concepts and Principles**

IT project governance refers to the framework that governs the planning, execution, and delivery of IT projects within an organization. It involves establishing policies, procedures, and guidelines that ensure project success, alignment with organizational goals, and efficient use of resources.

The key principles of IT project governance include:

  • Governance**: Ensuring that IT projects are aligned with organizational strategy and objectives 2. **Risk Management**: Identifying, assessing, and mitigating risks associated with IT projects 3. **Quality Assurance**: Ensuring that IT projects meet quality standards and requirements 4. **Compliance**: Ensuring that IT projects comply with regulatory and industry requirements 5. **Monitoring and Control**: Continuously monitoring and controlling IT project progress to ensure alignment with goals and objectives
