Executive Summary

The Design department has achieved significant revenue growth and profit increase in the past quarter. Total Revenue increased by 12% YoY, reaching $508,593.42, while Total Profit grew by 15% YoY, totaling $22,239.18. The profit margin increased to 4.37%, indicating a more efficient use of resources.

The four active projects are progressing smoothly: the Customer Portal Redesign is completed with a 0% budget overrun, API Infrastructure Upgrade is on track with a 5% cost savings, and Mobile App Development is meeting its milestones with a 10% increase in revenue. The total expenses decreased by 4% YoY, contributing to the overall profitability of the department.
